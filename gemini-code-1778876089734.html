The Rock

Gold acts as your insurance. It thrives during currency devaluation, high inflation, and periods of global geopolitical crisis.

The Paper

Cash is your optionality. It provides stability during deflation and the liquid "dry powder" needed to buy assets when they are cheap.

The Equity

Stocks are your growth engine. They capture the long-term productivity and innovation of the world's most successful companies.

The Smooth Ride

By blending non-correlated assets, you eliminate the violent drawdowns of a 100% stock portfolio while preserving steady, long-term compounding power.

The Rebalancing Protocol

Set Your Date

Choose one day a year (like your birthday or Jan 1st) to look at your portfolio. No more, no less.

Identify the Winner

If Stocks had a 50% gain, they might now represent 45% of your total wealth. This is your "Winner."

Reset the Weights

Sell the Winner and buy the asset that is currently lagging until all three are back to exactly 33.3%.
